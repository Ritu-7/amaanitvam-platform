info General Questions

What is Amaanitvam Foundation? expand_more
Amaanitvam Foundation is a registered non-profit organization dedicated to community development, education, and social welfare. Our mission is to empower underprivileged communities through sustainable programs and dedicated volunteer efforts.
Where do you operate? expand_more
We currently operate across multiple states with our primary headquarters in the capital region. We have regional chapters that manage local community projects.
How can I participate? expand_more
There are many ways to get involved! You can volunteer your time, apply for our internship programs, participate in our events, or support our causes through donations. Check our 'Get Involved' page for current opportunities.

volunteer_activism Volunteer FAQs

How do I become a volunteer? expand_more
To become a volunteer, simply fill out the volunteer application form available on our website. Our team will review your application and contact you for an onboarding session based on our current requirements.
Is volunteering remote or onsite? expand_more
We offer both remote and onsite volunteering opportunities. When you apply, you can specify your preference and availability. Onsite roles usually involve field work or event management, while remote roles might include digital advocacy, content creation, or research.
Do volunteers receive certificates? expand_more
Yes, volunteers who successfully complete their committed hours and responsibilities receive a formal certificate of appreciation detailing their contribution.

school Internship FAQs

How do I apply? expand_more
Internship openings are posted quarterly on our Careers/Opportunities page. You can apply by submitting your resume and a brief statement of purpose through our online portal.
Is the internship paid? expand_more
Our internships are currently unpaid, volunteer-based roles designed to provide practical experience, mentorship, and a platform to contribute to meaningful social causes. We do, however, cover pre-approved field expenses.
How long does it last? expand_more
Internship durations typically range from 4 to 12 weeks, depending on the specific program or project requirements. We offer flexible hours to accommodate students.

event Event FAQs

How do I register for events? expand_more
You can register for upcoming events through the 'Events' page on our website. Simply select the event you're interested in and click the 'Register' button to fill out the form.
Are events free to attend? expand_more
Most of our community awareness and volunteer events are free. However, some specialized workshops or fundraising galas may have a registration fee to cover costs or support our causes.
Can I volunteer during events? expand_more
Absolutely! We always need event-day volunteers for logistics, registration, and coordination. You can sign up specifically for event volunteering through our volunteer portal.
Will I receive participation certificates? expand_more
Yes, we provide digital participation certificates for all attendees of our workshops and seminars. These are usually emailed within 7-10 working days after the event.

verified Certificate Verification

How can I verify my certificate? expand_more
You can verify your certificate by entering the unique Certificate ID on our 'Certificate Verification' portal. The system will display the recipient's name, program details, and issue date.
When are certificates issued? expand_more
Certificates for volunteers and interns are typically issued within 15 days of the successful completion of their tenure and submission of the final report.
What details are required for verification? expand_more
You only need the unique alphanumeric Certificate ID printed at the bottom of your certificate. No personal login is required for verification.
Who should I contact if verification fails? expand_more
If you encounter any issues with verification, please email our support team at support@amaanitvam.org with a scanned copy of your certificate.

volunteer_activism Donations & Support

How can I donate? expand_more
You can donate online through our secure payment gateway using credit/debit cards, UPI, or net banking. We also accept bank transfers and cheques.
Are donations tax deductible? expand_more
Yes, all donations to Amaanitvam Foundation are eligible for tax deduction under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act. You will receive a tax-exempt receipt via email.
How are donations utilized? expand_more
Donations are primarily used to fund our educational programs, health camps, and community infrastructure projects. We maintain 100% transparency and publish annual financial reports.
Can organizations partner with the foundation? expand_more
Yes, we welcome corporate partnerships and CSR collaborations. Organizations can partner with us for specific projects or long-term community development initiatives.
