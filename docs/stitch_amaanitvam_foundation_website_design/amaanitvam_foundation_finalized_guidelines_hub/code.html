Frequently Referenced Policies

Quick answers regarding our most common procedural questions.

Attendance Requirements for Volunteers expand_more

Volunteers are expected to attend at least 75% of committed shifts. If you cannot make a scheduled shift, please notify your team lead at least 24 hours in advance via the official communication channels. Extended leaves of absence should be requested formally through the volunteer portal.

Certificate Issuance Criteria expand_more

Certificates of participation are issued only after completing a minimum of 40 hours of active service within a 6-month period. All hours must be logged and verified by a program coordinator. Certificates are generated automatically at the end of each quarter and can be verified via our online portal.

Expense Reimbursement Protocol expand_more

Pre-approved expenses incurred during official foundation duties will be reimbursed. Original receipts must be submitted along with a completed reimbursement form within 14 days of the expense. General travel to and from standard volunteer sites is not covered unless specifically authorized for a special event.

Media Release & Photography expand_more

Only designated media coordinators are authorized to take photographs during sensitive outreach programs. All external media sharing requires prior consent forms from beneficiaries. Members must not post identifying information of minors on any public platform under any circumstances.

Need Clarification?

If you have questions about a specific policy or cannot find the guideline you are looking for, our support team is ready to assist you.
