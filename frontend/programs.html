Beneficiary Story

A Life Transformed

"Before Amaanitvam came to our village, I had never touched a computer. Today, I teach digital literacy to younger students and dream of becoming an engineer. The foundation didn't just give us books — they gave us belief."

Priya Sharma — Rural Education Initiative Beneficiary, Telangana

Priya joined our Rural Education Initiative in 2019. After completing the digital skills track, she became a peer tutor and is now pursuing higher education with a foundation scholarship.
