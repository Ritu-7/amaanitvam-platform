Process

How It Works

  1. 1

    Locate Certificate ID

    Find the unique certificate ID printed on the bottom left of the official document.

  2. 2

    Enter ID & Submit

    Type the ID into the verification form above and click the Verify button.

  3. 3

    Receive Instant Result

    The system checks our secure database and displays whether the certificate is authentic.

  4. 4

    Contact for Support

    If you encounter issues, reach out via our contact page for verification support.
